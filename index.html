About the game

What is Wishgranted?

Wishgranted is a free online puzzle game where you write a single wish that must satisfy an impossible genie's growing list of rules. It belongs to the rule-cascade puzzle genre — the same family as The Password Game — where satisfying one condition can quietly break another, and the comedy is in watching your sentence twist itself into knots.

There is nothing to download and no account to create. Wishgranted runs entirely in your browser on desktop or mobile, it is completely free, and every keystroke stays on your device — nothing you type is ever sent anywhere.

How to Play

  1. Type a wish into the box. The genie reveals his conditions one at a time.
  2. Each rule is checked live as you type — passed rules are struck through, unmet rules wait in red.
  3. Satisfy every active rule and the genie grants you the next, harder one.
  4. Stuck? You have 5 hints for the whole game — spend them wisely. The genie reacts to everything you do.
  5. Clear all 10 levels to reach the ending.

Classic Mode

One wish, ten levels, thirty rules. Your sentence is never cleared — it grows and mutates across every level until a single line satisfies the genie completely. The wish you finish with is the prize.

Quick Mode

Ten bite-sized levels, a fresh wish each time, no commitment. Clear all ten and you unlock the Final Wish — a 27-condition gauntlet built into one sentence, hiding a contradiction the genie dares you to find.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Wishgranted free to play?

Yes. Wishgranted is completely free. There are no ads, no accounts, and no payments — just open the page and play.

Do I need to download or install anything?

No. Wishgranted runs entirely in your web browser on desktop or mobile. There is nothing to download or install.

Is Wishgranted like The Password Game?

It shares the rule-cascade mechanic — satisfying one rule can break another — but Wishgranted is themed around a deadpan genie, splits the experience into 10 curated levels, and ends with a Final Wish gauntlet instead of one endless list.

How many levels and rules does Wishgranted have?

There are 10 hand-curated levels drawing on a pool of 56 rules, plus a Final Wish in Quick Mode that stacks 27 conditions into a single sentence.

Can I play Wishgranted on mobile?

Yes. Wishgranted is mobile-first and works in any modern phone or tablet browser. Your progress is saved automatically.

Does Wishgranted save my progress?

Yes. Progress, your current wish, hints, and your chosen mode are all saved in your browser's local storage. Nothing you type ever leaves your device.

What is the Final Wish in Wishgranted?

The Final Wish is the climax of Quick Mode: after clearing all 10 levels you face a single wish that must satisfy 27 conditions at once — including one impossible contradiction the genie expects you to find.
